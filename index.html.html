Class Levels

A Class for Every Little Swimmer

From babies to confident swimmers — we have the right level for your child, right where they are today.

🌟

Starfish

6 – 12 Months

Sensory and motor stimulation in water. We build water confidence and comfort for your baby from the very beginning.

🐠

Seahorse

13 – 24 Months

Your toddler learns to hold their breath and begins using arms and legs to move consciously through the water.

🦆

Duck

25 – 48 Months

Developing independence! Children master breath control and learn to swim a short distance with growing confidence.

🏊

Learn-to-Swim

4 Years & Up

Full stroke technique — freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly. Your child swims safely and confidently.

Frequently Asked

Your Questions, Answered

Is my 6-month-old baby really old enough for swimming classes?↓
Yes! Babies as young as 6 months benefit enormously from water exposure. Our Starfish level is designed specifically for babies — focused on sensory stimulation, motor development, and early water confidence. Everything is done gently and safely.
What if my child is scared of water?↓
This is one of the most common reasons parents choose Swimtime. Coach Dimas is trained to work with water-anxious children using patience and play — never force. Fear is just a starting point, not a barrier.
How small are the class sizes?↓
We keep class sizes intentionally small so every child receives personal attention. Your child will never get lost in a large group — this is core to how we teach.
Who is the coach?↓
Swimtime Aquatics is founded and led by Dimas K. Hudaja, a certified swim coach with experience from Singapore. Dimas brings a deep belief in gentle, discovery-based learning and personally leads every class.
How do I enroll or find out more?↓
The easiest way is to chat with us on WhatsApp! Coach Dimas will answer any questions, help identify the right level for your child, and walk you through next steps — no pressure at all.
